I am Emmet Krohn

I started doing photography around the end of 2024, when I found mom's 2011 camera.

Since then, I've been taking photos of nature, landscapes, animals, sports, or anything interesting I see.

My favorite subjects are wildlife and landscapes, but I also heavily enjoy shooting track or other sports.

I use a Canon EOS Rebel T3I with a 55-250mm f/4 lens, as well as a 14-25mm f/3.5 lens for wide shots.

Recently I have picked up film photography as well, using a Canon EOS 650 as well as a recently purchased Pentax ME with a f/1.7 50mm lens.
